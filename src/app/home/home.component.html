How and how long meals can be stored?

We deliver fresh dishes, and the technology of packaging and storage of meals used by us allows the dishes to remain fresh and tasty in the refrigerator for about a week.

You should follow simple rules so that the dishes do not deteriorate prematurely:

  • Use only dry and clean appliances
  • Close containers tightly
  • Store containers in the refrigerator +3 - +5 degrees
  • Reheat meals in portions as needed
